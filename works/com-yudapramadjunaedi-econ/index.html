Deskripsi
WED provide information for 198 countries including historical data for more than 400.000 economic indicators, exchange rates, stock market indexes, government bond yields and commodity prices. Our data is based on official sources.

Bookmark
+ There are more than 400.000 indicators. For easy access, you can bookmark your favorite indicator
+ This screen contain all your bookmarked indicator

Usage
+ Tap bookmark icon to bookmark
+ Tap export icon to export data and mail it

+ Table can be scrolled horizontally. No need to change orientation
